Agimat Stories

A strike of lightning is needed to fuse ordinary minerals into amulets or agimats. Gifted through sacred prayers, it is believed that whomever holds an agimat will be able to harness at will superhuman abilities.

Rooted in Filipino mythology and religion, Tribe Agimat Mojos wield unique powers.
